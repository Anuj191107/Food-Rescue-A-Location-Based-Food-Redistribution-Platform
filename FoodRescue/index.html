No Food Should Go to Waste

Every day, surplus food is wasted while many struggle for a meal. Our platform connects food donors with volunteers to ensure food reaches those in need.

By submitting a donation, you accept that your contact and location details may be shared with volunteers for food collection.

This initiative is built to encourage responsible food redistribution and empower volunteers to take immediate action locally.

This website is developed by FY students of KIT's College of Engineering as an academic and social-impact project.
